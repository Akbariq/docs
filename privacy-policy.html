Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Snipbite ("we", "us", "our") operates social media accounts on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube where we publish short-form recipe videos. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our services.

1. Information We Collect

Snipbite is a content publishing service. We do not collect personal information from viewers of our videos. The only data we access is:

  • Social media platform data: We use official platform APIs (Meta Graph API, TikTok API) to publish video content to our own Pages and accounts. This requires access tokens that authorize posting to our own accounts only.
  • Public engagement metrics: We may access aggregated, non-personal engagement data (view counts, like counts) for our own published content to measure performance.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or process personal data from any social media users.
  • We do not access other users' profiles, messages, friend lists, or private content.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies on any website.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.

3. How We Use Platform APIs

We use the following platform APIs solely to publish content to our own accounts:

  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram): We use the Meta Graph API and Instagram Graph API to publish Reels to our own Facebook Pages and Instagram Professional accounts. Permissions used: pages_manage_posts, pages_read_engagement, instagram_content_publish, instagram_basic.
  • TikTok: We use the TikTok Content Posting API to publish videos to our own TikTok accounts.

4. Data Storage & Security

API access tokens required for publishing are stored securely on our local systems and are never shared or exposed publicly. We do not operate servers or databases that store user data.

5. Third-Party Services

Our video production pipeline uses the following services to generate content:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — for generating recipe scripts
  • Runway — for AI video generation
  • ElevenLabs — for text-to-speech voiceovers

These services process recipe content only and do not receive any personal or user data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
